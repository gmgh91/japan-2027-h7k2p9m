Onsen town. Mt Fuji views, volcanic valleys, and the slow-down day before Kyoto.

Where we could stay

Travel

Tokyo → Hakone + Open-Air Museum

Odakyu Romancecar from Shinjuku (~85 min). Lunch in Yumoto. Afternoon: Hakone Open-Air Museum. Check in, onsen + kaiseki dinner.

7Sun Feb
Anchor

The Hakone Loop

Tozan switchback railway → cable car → ropeway over Owakudani (volcanic valley, black eggs) → pirate ship across Lake Ashi (Fuji views) → Hakone Shrine (torii in the water) → bus back.
